A word from the Vera Institute...

The Vera Institute reminds us that comparing states' per inmate cost should be avoided. Lower expenses may lead to poorer outcomes in terms of safety and recidivism.

The Vera Institute:

Ways to lower spending without reducing public safety include: modifying sentencing and release policies, strengthening strategies to reduce recidivism, and boosting operating efficiency.
